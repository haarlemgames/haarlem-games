About Us

Ideas crafted into games.

At Haarlem Games, we use modern AI-assisted development and lean production methods to keep costs low and creativity high. This gives us room to experiment, test quickly, and focus on what matters most: gameplay that feels good from the first click.


Our home is Haarlem, a city full of history, craft, and charm. That same feeling inspires how we build: with care, curiosity, and just enough Dutch stubbornness to keep improving.
